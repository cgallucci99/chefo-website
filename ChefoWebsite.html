About

Chef-O-Nette is an Upper Arlington tradition. We have been in business since 1955, serving breakfast, lunch and dinner to generations of UA residents.

Menu

The Chef-o-Nette has a breakfast menu, as well as a lunch and dinner menu. Breakfast is served all day. Call in and see what our daily lunch and dinner specials are!

(click on the image to see the menu)

Drive-Thru

Chef-O-Nette is home to one of the very first drive-thrus in the nation, and it is still in operation today! To place a carry-out order, simply call (614) 488-8444 or drive up to the window. Typical wait times range from 5-15 minutes

Location

Located at 2090 Tremont Center, just off Tremont road, Chef-O-Nette is easily accessible from most of Upper Arlington
